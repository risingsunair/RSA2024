System Diagnostic

Includes:

  • Examining the thermostat to ensure it is functioning properly.
  • Checking the system controls and start-up cycle.
  • Tightening all the electrical connections.
  • Checking for correct current and voltage on all motors.
  • Inspecting the condensate drain.
  • Checking and adjusting the cooling system’s refrigerant level.

A/C Tune Up

Includes:

  • Check and adjust thermostat ad needed
  • Check filter
  • Check voltages
  • Check all wiring and connections
  • Check starting contactor
  • Check all capacitors
  • Check and measure all motor amperages
  • Check operation of unit safety controls
  • Check super heat
  • Check evaporator coil condition
  • Check for rubbing/vibrating refrigerant lines
  • Check condenser coils
  • Check the air temperatura across evaporator coil
  • Check fan blades for cracks
  • Check condenser temperature rise
  • Check condensate drain or pump
  • Check fuse disconnects
  • Check condition of ductwork
